# Why use a Terminal App?

Modern web browsers are massive, resource-heavy environments that serve as the primary gateway for data collection. Most "Modern" IPTV web apps are built on top of browsers, bringing with them a tail of trackers, cookies, and unnecessary bloat.

In a TUI (Text User Interface), there are no images to decode for the interface, no layout engines to compute complex CSS, and no garbage collection pauses from 50MB of JavaScript.

- **Instant Boot**: From terminal to TV in under 2 seconds.
- **Low Latency**: Every microsecond saved in UI rendering is a microsecond faster you get to your content.
- **Low Overhead**: Uses less than 1% of the RAM required by a modern Chrome tab.
